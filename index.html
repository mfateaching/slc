Hello and welcome! I’m Afifa, and I’m delighted to share a little about my journey as a pre-primary educator. With 12 years of experience in nurturing young minds, I’m passionate about creating a warm, engaging, and stimulating environment for early learners.

My Philosophy
I believe that every child is unique and has their own way of discovering the world. My teaching approach is centered around fostering a love for learning through play, exploration, and creativity. I strive to build a supportive and inclusive classroom where children feel safe, valued, and excited to explore new concepts.

Education & Experience
I hold a degree from Asian College Of Teachers and have completed Phonics training from Sparkle Academy . My experience ranges from teaching kids from pre & primary to 8th Standard. I’m constantly seeking new methods and strategies to enhance my teaching and stay updated with the latest in early childhood education.

What to Expect
In our classroom, you can expect a vibrant mix of activities designed to stimulate young minds and encourage social and emotional growth. From hands-on projects and storytime to outdoor play and group activities, I ensure that each day is filled with learning opportunities that cater to diverse interests and developmental stages.

Partnership with Parents
I believe that open communication between home and school is key to a child’s success. I’m committed to keeping parents informed and involved through regular updates, parent-teacher conferences, and collaborative events.

Thank you for visiting my site. I’m excited about the possibility of working with your child and being a part of their early learning journey. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to learn more about our program!
